## Dr. Eve Rutherford

Dentist

Dr. Eve Rutherford is a graduate of the University of Washington School of Dentistry and received a Bachelor of Arts with Honors in Biology form Scripps College in Claremont, California. Prior to entering dental school, she worked in the Department of Pathology at the University of Washington studying connective tissue disorders. Dr. Eve was raised in Kalispell, Montana where she graduated from Flathead High School. I am dedicated to improving the oral health and overall health of my patients. My goal is to prevent oral disease and educate patients about the links between a healthy mouth and a healthy body. I love being a dentist and serving my patients through conscious and mindful care. My dedication to improving oral health in the community extends to work I do outside the office. I have served on the Board of Directors for Washington Dental Service Foundation since 2015. During 2016 I served on a task force with Washington State Health Care Authority that seeks to improve benefits for Adult Dental Medicaid.. I am also active with the ABCD Program (Access to Baby and Child Dentistry), and Affiliate Faculty for the University of Washington Department of Pediatric Dentistry.I have two active sons who keep me on my toes. We enjoy hiking the trials in Snohomish County and biking on Centennial Trail. My husband and I enjoy golfing and going out for sushi.On my own time, I love to go running and read books.

Dr. Rutherford practices Mondays, Tuesdays, Wednesdays, and Thursdays.

## Dr. Rachel Greene

Dentist

Dr. Rachel (Krell) Greene is a graduate of the University of Michigan School of Dentistry and received Bachelor of Arts with Honors in Science, Technology and Society from the University of Puget Sound. Dr. Greene was born and raised in Snohomish and is happy to have returned to her hometown to practice in the community she grew up in. In addition to practicing dentistry, Dr. Greene is a professor at the University of Washington School of Dentistry working as the Clinical Director of Western Washington for the Service Learning Program and as a Clinical Instructor in the student patient clinic.

Dentistry is my passion and I enjoy empowering others to have a healthy mouth. I strive to help all patients have a positive dental experience and feel at ease coming to the dentist. I empathize with those who have dental anxiety and am more than willing to work with you to determine the best way to provide you with comfortable care. As a detail oriented person, my restorative focus is on precision and I strive for excellence in all my work. I look forward to meeting you and finding out how our office can best serve your dental care needs.

Dr. Greene practices on Thursdays and Fridays by appointment.

## Dr. Samiksha Gulrajani

Dentist

Originally from Cincinnati, Ohio, she completed her undergraduate studies in Mathematics and Neuroscience at the University of Pittsburgh, graduating with honors. She also earned a minor in Chemistry before completing her Doctor of Dental Medicine (DMD) degree at the University of Pittsburgh School of Dental Medicine. Following her education, Dr. Gulrajani further honed her skills during her residency in Advanced Education in General Dentistry (AEGD), where she treated complex cases both at the University and at a local urgent care clinic. She specializes in providing comprehensive dental care, with a particular focus on patients with special needs.​

Outside of her professional work, Dr. Gulrajani enjoys spending time watching for orcas and baking delicious desserts.​

Dr. Gulrajani practices on Monday, Tuesday, and Wednesday
