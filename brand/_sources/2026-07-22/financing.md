# Financing Options

We believe quality dental care should be accessible and affordable for everyone. That’s why we offer flexible financing options through CareCredit and Cherry, two trusted providers that let you split your treatment costs into manageable monthly payments. Whether you’re planning a routine procedure or a more extensive treatment, financing can help you get the care you need without delay. Both options feature a quick application process, and approval decisions are often made within minutes. If you have questions or need help applying, our front desk team is always happy to walk you through the process and find the best solution for your budget.​

| Feature | CareCredit | Cherry |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Type of Financing | Healthcare credit card | “Buy Now, Pay Later” plan |
| Approval Time | Instant approval in most cases | Instant approval in most cases |
| Payment Terms | Short and long term options (6-60 months) | Flexible monthly payment plans (split up to 12 months) |
| Interest Options | 0% interest promotional periods available | 0% APR options available based on credit |
| Credit Check | Yes | Soft credit check (no impact on score) |
| Best For | Larger treatment plans or ongoing care | Smaller procedures or one-time costs |
