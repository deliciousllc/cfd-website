# TMJ

Helping You Get Relief from TMJ

If you're experiencing jaw soreness, clicking or popping, facial pain or frequent headaches, you may be suffering from TMJ disorder-also known as TMD. We offer advanced diagnostics and customized treatment options to being you long-lasting relief. We are here to help find custom solutions that work!

​

We offer

CBCT Imaging to get a clear, 3D view of your jaw joint, allowing us to pinpoint the cause of your discomfort.

Occlusal Orthotic Devices to align your bite and relieve pressure on your jaw joint

Botox for muscle related TMJ issues to relax overactive jaw muscles
